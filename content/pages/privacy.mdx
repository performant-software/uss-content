      # Privacy Policy


      *Last updated: April 1, 2026*


      Your privacy is important to us. Universities Studying Slavery (USS) is
      committed to being thoughtful about the personal information we collect and
      transparent about how we use it. This Privacy Policy explains how we
      collect, use, and share information when you use our website and services.


      ## Information We Collect


      We collect information about you only when we have a reason to do so — for
      example, to provide our services, to communicate with you, or to improve
      the website. We collect information from two sources: information you
      provide to us directly, and information collected automatically through the
      operation of our services.


      ### Information You Provide to Us


      **Account information:** If you create an account, we collect the
      information you provide during registration, such as your name and email
      address.


      **Communications:** When you contact us with questions, feedback, or
      support requests, we collect the information you include in those
      communications.


      **Content contributions:** If you contribute content to the site (such as
      posts, annotations, or other materials), we collect the information you
      provide in those contributions.


      ### Information We Collect Automatically


      **Log information:** Like most websites, we collect information that web
      browsers and servers typically make available, including browser type, IP
      address, language preference, referring site, the date and time of access,
      and operating system.


      **Usage information:** We collect information about how you use the site,
      including which pages you visit, search queries you enter, and how you
      interact with search results and maps. We use this information to
      understand how people use our services and to make improvements.


      **Cookies and similar technologies:** We use cookies and similar
      technologies to help identify and track visitors, remember preferences, and
      understand site usage. You can set your browser to reject cookies, though
      some site features may not function properly without them.


      ## How and Why We Use Information


      We use the information we collect to:


      - **Provide our services.** Operate the website, serve search results,
      display maps and cultural heritage records, and maintain your account.

      - **Improve our services.** Monitor and analyze usage to identify issues,
      develop new features, and improve the user experience.

      - **Communicate with you.** Respond to your questions, send important
      notices about the site, and provide updates about USS activities when you
      have opted in.

      - **Ensure security.** Detect and prevent malicious, fraudulent, or illegal
      activity, and protect the rights and safety of USS and our users.


      ## Sharing Information


      We share information about you in limited circumstances and with
      appropriate safeguards:


      **Service providers:** We may share information with third-party vendors
      who help us operate our services, including hosting providers, search
      infrastructure (Typesense), content management services, and analytics
      tools. We require these vendors to protect your information consistent with
      this policy.


      **Legal requirements:** We may disclose information in response to a
      subpoena, court order, or other governmental request.


      **Protection of rights:** We may disclose information when we believe in
      good faith that disclosure is reasonably necessary to protect the rights or
      safety of USS, our users, or the public.


      **With your consent:** We may share information with your consent or at
      your direction.


      **Aggregated or de-identified information:** We may share information that
      has been aggregated or de-identified so that it can no longer reasonably be
      used to identify you.


      ## How Long We Keep Information


      We generally retain information about you for as long as we have a
      legitimate reason to do so. When the information is no longer needed, we
      will delete it or anonymize it.


      ## Security


      While no online service is 100% secure, we take reasonable measures to
      protect information about you against unauthorized access, use, alteration,
      or destruction.


      ## Your Rights


      Depending on where you are located, you may have certain rights regarding
      your personal information.


      ### European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)


      If you are located in a country under the scope of the GDPR, you have the
      right to:


      - Request access to your personal data

      - Request correction or deletion of your personal data

      - Object to our use and processing of your personal data

      - Request that we limit our use and processing of your personal data

      - Request portability of your personal data


      You also have the right to make a complaint to a government supervisory
      authority.


      ### US Privacy Laws


      Residents of certain US states (including California, Colorado,
      Connecticut, Utah, and Virginia) may have additional rights regarding their
      personal information, including the right to access, delete, and opt out of
      the sale or sharing of personal information.


      We do not sell your personal information. To the extent that sharing
      information with analytics or advertising vendors constitutes a "sale" or
      "share" under applicable law, you may opt out by adjusting your browser
      cookie settings or using the Global Privacy Control (GPC) signal.


      ## Changes to This Policy


      We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of
      any significant changes by posting a notice on the site.


      ## Contact Us


      If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to exercise your
      rights, please contact us at ussconsortium@gmail.com.
